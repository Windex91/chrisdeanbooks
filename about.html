About

Short bio

Christopher Dean is an Australian Indie author, his debut novel The Glass Tapestry focuses on the power of choice, and how the consequences of our actions are always ready to bite back.

Long bio

Christopher Dean writes Specualtive Fiction and Fantasy. With three more projects currently on the go, 2026 is shaping up to be a big year for him. With the sequel to The Glass Tapestry, A new series titled A Saga of Gods and Kings, and a western reaching into the frontier of American outlaws, there is bound to be something for everyone to read.

When he’s not writing, he’s usually thinking about writing, whether it's further world‑building, the strange logic of alternate realities, or just how far he can push his characters before they break.

What to expect

  • Speculative premises with clear stakes
  • Character‑driven tension and moral trade‑offs
  • Deeply vivd worlds, emotions, and characters you just love to hate.
