About

Short bio

Christopher Dean is an Australian science fiction author writing high‑concept stories about parallel universes, identity, and the lives we might have lived.

Long bio

Christopher Dean writes science fiction that blends big ideas with grounded emotion. His work explores the ripple effects of choice—how one moment can split a life into countless possibilities, and how longing can become its own kind of gravity.

When he’s not writing, he’s usually thinking about systems, world‑building, and the strange logic of alternate realities.

What to expect

  • Speculative premises with clear stakes
  • Character‑driven tension and moral trade‑offs
  • Series continuity, reading order, and updates via newsletter
