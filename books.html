The Glass Tapestry

Book 1

Ravi Qadir spent his life keeping a secret - he can see infinite versions of reality layered over his own, and take whatever he wants from them. What began as a childhood game, became the greatest magic show act on earth. But no amount of fame and fortune can quiet the ache for the one thing he can't take - a life of love and peace he's only ever glimpsed in another world When jealousy and despair drive him to a shocking and impossible act, Ravi learns he can do more than just take from other worlds, he can cross the boundaries between them. Now trapped in a reality that isn't his, hunted by the consequences of his own actions, Ravi must navigate a fragile web of worlds - and the darker truths that hide in their reflections. A story of ambition, longing, and the dangerous power of choice, The Glass Tapestry weaves a thrilling journey through the multiverse - where a single pulled thread, could change everything
